# QuoteWinBack Pilot Checklist

## Before the call

- Pick one niche and local area.
- Find 50 businesses with clear contact details.
- Look for lead leakage signals: slow response reviews, no booking link, weak contact form, unanswered social comments, old weather-damage posts.
- Send cold email and call the same day.

## Discovery call

- Confirm average job value.
- Ask how they track missed calls and unclosed quotes.
- Ask who owns follow-up after a quote is sent.
- Ask whether they can export old leads from their CRM, phone provider, forms, or spreadsheet.
- Offer the no-risk recovery scan.

## Onboarding

- Get business name.
- Get booking link or preferred callback number.
- Get the last 60-90 days of unclosed enquiries.
- Confirm whether SMS, email, or manual calling is allowed.
- Confirm opt-out language and who approves messages.

## Delivery

- Upload or enter enquiries in the prototype.
- Review hot and warm recovery targets.
- Edit drafts for the business voice.
- Get owner approval.
- Send the first batch manually.
- Log replies, bookings, and lost reasons.

## Success report

- Total leads scanned.
- Recovery opportunities found.
- Messages sent.
- Replies received.
- Call-backs, inspections, or appointments booked.
- Estimated recovered pipeline.
- Recommended monthly follow-up plan.
