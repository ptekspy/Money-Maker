# LetDue profit plan

## Commercial objective

Reach the first 10 paying landlords before expanding the product beyond one to three properties. The only north-star metric is active paid subscriptions; audits and pilots matter only when they move landlords toward that outcome.

## Offer

- Free, no-account property deadline audit.
- Fourteen-day pilot for up to three properties, with no card required.
- Founding plan: £29 per year for up to three properties.
- Raise the public price to £49 per year after the first 10 paying customers. Existing founding customers keep £29 while continuously subscribed.

## First 30-day targets

- 30 completed property audits.
- 10 pilots with at least one real deadline saved.
- 6 pilots with at least one certificate uploaded.
- 3 paid founding customers.
- 1 certificate provider or landlord group actively referring landlords.

## Funnel measurements

Track these weekly:

1. Landing-page visitors.
2. Example audits and real audits completed.
3. Pilots started.
4. Pilots that save a real deadline.
5. Pilots that upload a certificate.
6. Checkout starts.
7. Paid conversions.
8. Cancellations and stated reason.

The activation event is **one real property with one real deadline saved**. The stronger activation event is **one certificate uploaded**.

## Acquisition order

1. Finish the personalised first-batch follow-ups already scheduled for 19 July 2026.
2. Ask interested landlords to set up one real property during a 15-minute call rather than merely agreeing to “try it later”.
3. Recruit one EPC/EICR/gas-certificate provider to include the free audit link in its completion email.
4. Offer York RLA or a local landlord group a practical certificate-deadline clinic. Do not request its member list.
5. Publish useful, location-specific deadline guides only after the activation and payment funnel has converted at least one customer.

## Conversion rules

- The product sends pilot-ending reminders seven days, three days and on the final day.
- Every pilot dashboard contains the £29 upgrade action.
- An expired pilot becomes read-only but retains its records and upgrade action.
- Ask every non-converting pilot one question: “What stopped this being worth £29 a year?”
- Do not add portfolio features until three prospects independently cite the three-property limit.

## Weekly operating rhythm

### Monday

- Review new audits, pilots, uploads and payments.
- Contact activated pilots that have not saved a real deadline.

### Wednesday

- Conduct product calls and partner demonstrations.
- Record the exact objection or useful outcome from each conversation.

### Friday

- Review conversion rates and support issues.
- Ship one improvement tied to an observed funnel problem.
- Stop any channel that has produced no replies after 20 well-targeted contacts.

## Immediate deployment prerequisite

The conversion release is built and verified locally. Deploy it through the AWS/SST credentials that currently own the live LetDue stage, then complete one Stripe test checkout before directing more pilots to the product.
