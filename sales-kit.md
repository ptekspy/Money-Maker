# QuoteWinBack Sales Kit

## Target niche

Start with roofing, damp proofing, plumbing, boiler, and window/door companies in Bradford and West Yorkshire. They have urgent enquiries, high-value jobs, old quotes, missed calls, and paid marketplace leads from places like Checkatrade, MyBuilder, Rated People, Bark, Google, and Facebook.

## Positioning

QuoteWinBack recovers the trade enquiries a company already paid for.

This is not an ad agency, SEO offer, or CRM replacement. It is a follow-up engine for missed calls, stale quotes, and ghosted enquiries.

## Cold email

Subject: You are probably sitting on unclosed jobs

Hey {{first_name}},

Most trade businesses have old quotes, missed calls, and paid enquiries that never got followed up with.

I built a simple recovery system that finds those warm enquiries and follows up until they book, reply, or say no.

If you send your last 60-90 days of unclosed enquiries, I will show you the recoverable pipeline before you pay.

Worth a look?

## Follow-up email

Subject: quick follow-up

Hey {{first_name}},

The reason I am asking is simple: one recovered job can pay for this for months.

I am happy to run a no-pressure recovery scan on old quotes or missed enquiries and show you what is sitting there.

Want me to take a look?

## Short contact form message

Hi, I am local to Bradford and testing a missed-enquiry recovery service for roofers.

It helps recover old quotes, missed calls, website forms, and Checkatrade/MyBuilder/Bark leads that went cold.

If you send the last 60-90 days of unclosed enquiries, I will show the recoverable pipeline before you pay. If there are not at least 5 warm opportunities, there is no charge.

Worth a quick look?

## Qualification questions

- How do you currently track missed calls and old quotes?
- How many enquiries or quote requests do you get in a normal month?
- Who follows up after a quote is sent?
- What is an average booked job worth?
- Do you use Checkatrade, MyBuilder, Bark, Rated People, Google, or Facebook for leads?
- Can you export unclosed enquiries from your CRM, phone system, email, or spreadsheet?

## Pilot close

Send me the last 60-90 days of unclosed quotes, missed calls, or form enquiries. I will build the recovery queue and show you exactly who is worth following up with. If there are not at least 5 warm opportunities, you do not pay.

## Objections

"We already follow up."

Totally. This is for the enquiries that slipped through anyway. Every busy trade business has them.

"We use a CRM."

Great. This sits on top of it. The CRM stores the enquiry; QuoteWinBack finds the money that got stale.

"I do not want AI texting customers."

Good instinct. The first version uses approval mode. You approve every message before it goes out.

"How do I know it worked?"

We track replies, booked appointments, booked jobs, and estimated recovered revenue from the recovery queue.
