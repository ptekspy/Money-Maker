# QuoteWinBack Brand Kit

## Recommended company/product name

QuoteWinBack

## Positioning line

Recover old quotes and missed enquiries before they go cold.

## Short description

QuoteWinBack helps roofing and trade businesses recover money from old quotes, missed calls, website forms, and paid marketplace enquiries that were never properly followed up.

## Domain status

Chosen domain: `quotewinback.co.uk`.

Use this as the public domain and email base. Registrar/Nominet ownership and DNS still need to be configured outside the repo before custom-domain launch.

Manual checks:

- Nominet lookup: `https://www.nominet.uk/lookup/`
- 123 Reg: `https://www.123-reg.co.uk/domain-names/`
- Cloudflare Registrar: `https://www.cloudflare.com/products/registrar/`
- Namecheap: `https://www.namecheap.com/domains/registration/`

## Tone

- Local
- Plain English
- No hype
- No "AI automation agency" language
- No guaranteed revenue claims
- Lead with old quotes, missed enquiries, and paid lead waste

## Colour palette

- Ink: `#102820`
- Recovery green: `#176b4f`
- Warm lead: `#f2b84b`
- Paper: `#f5f7f8`
- White: `#ffffff`

## Logo concept

The logo should be simple: a speech bubble or quote mark turning back into an arrow.

The idea: old conversations coming back.

Use the included `logo.svg` as the first working logo.
