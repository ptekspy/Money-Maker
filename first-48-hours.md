# First 48 Hours

## Objective

Book 1 recovery scan with a Bradford/West Yorkshire roofer without making outbound phone calls.

## Day 1

1. Open `prospect-tracker.csv`.
2. Contact the first 30 prospects through website forms, public emails, Facebook pages, Google Business Profile message links, or directory enquiry buttons.
3. Use the Day 1 email/contact form message in `outreach-sequence.md`.
4. Mark each prospect as:
   - Message sent
   - Not interested
   - Asked for details
   - Interested
   - Needs follow-up
5. For anyone who asks for details, send the short example below.

## Day 2

1. Send the Day 3 follow-up to anyone marked "Message sent" or "Needs follow-up".
2. Reply quickly to anyone who asks for details.
3. Try to close the first scan with this:

   "Send me anything simple: spreadsheet, CRM export, screenshots, or a list of old quotes. I will build the recovery queue and show you what is worth chasing. If there are not at least 5 warm opportunities, there is no charge."

## What to ask for

- Old quote list
- Missed call log
- Website enquiry exports
- Checkatrade/MyBuilder/Bark enquiries
- Facebook/Google message enquiries
- Spreadsheet or screenshots if they do not have exports

## Example reply when they ask for details

The scan is simple. You send old unclosed enquiries from the last 60-90 days: spreadsheet, screenshots, CRM export, email enquiries, or marketplace leads. I sort them into hot/warm/ignore, draft follow-ups, and show the estimated recoverable pipeline. You approve anything before it is sent. If I do not find at least 5 warm recovery opportunities, there is no charge.

## First scan success metric

Find at least 5 warm recovery opportunities and get permission to send or approve follow-up messages.
