# Bradford Roofer No-Call Outreach Sequence

## Channel priority

Use channels that do not require live calling:

- Website contact forms
- Public business email addresses
- Facebook business page messages
- Google Business Profile message links where available
- LinkedIn message to owner/manager where obvious
- WhatsApp only when the business publicly presents it as a business contact channel

Avoid phone calls for first contact.

## Daily target

Message 30 roofers in one sitting across website forms, public emails, Facebook pages, and directory enquiry buttons. The goal is not to explain everything. The goal is to get one of these:

- "Send me details"
- "Yes, I have old quotes"
- "No, not interested"
- "Can you show me an example?"

## Qualification notes

Ask:

- How many quote requests do you get each month?
- Where do leads come from: phone, website, Checkatrade, MyBuilder, Bark, Facebook, Google?
- Who follows up after a quote is sent?
- Do you have old quotes or missed enquiries from the last 60-90 days?
- What is an average roof repair or replacement worth?
